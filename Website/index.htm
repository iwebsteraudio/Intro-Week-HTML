About Ian

My name is Ian Webster - Hello World.
I studied Music Production, Audio Production and Professional Broadcast Techniques at Salford University and I've worked for many years as a bass player and until 2016 my goal was to work as a session bass player, a music producer and songwriter. Well, instead of that I got a temporary job for Cubic Motion, which turned a little too permanent before Epic Games bought the company pre-pandemic. I played loads of gigs as a bass player which then fizzled out (like the rest of the world) during covid. Well, now I'm back since being laid off by Epic. I'm ready for the next chapter. Available for bookings as an acoustic guitar act, check out my repertoire.
